Privacy Policy for PatchPal

Last updated: January 3, 2026

Information We Collect

PatchPal collects the following information:

  • Child's first name: For personalization within the app
  • Patching session data: Start times, pause times, duration, and progress tracking
  • Device identifiers: For family sharing functionality across devices
  • Rewards and achievements: Stars earned, rewards unlocked, and milestone progress

How We Use Your Information

  • To track and display patching progress
  • To sync data across family devices via Apple's iCloud
  • To provide rewards, achievements, and motivational features
  • To enable family sharing functionality

Data Storage

All data is stored securely in Apple's iCloud service and remains under your control. We do not have access to your personal data stored in iCloud.

Data Sharing

Your data is only shared with family members you explicitly invite using the 6-digit code. We do not share your data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

PatchPal is designed for children under parental supervision. All data collection is done with parental consent and oversight.
