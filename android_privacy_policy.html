Privacy Policy for PatchPal (Android)

Last updated: March 2, 2026

1. Introduction

PatchPal ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the PatchPal for Android mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Child's name: For personalization within the app
  • Caretaker's name and relationship: To identify and customize the app interface
  • Eye information: Which eye is patched or tracked
  • Patching session data: Start times, pause times, duration, tags, and progress tracking
  • Visual acuity records: Optional eye test measurements and notes
  • Rewards and achievements: Stars earned, rewards unlocked, and milestone progress
  • Feedback messages: When you contact us through the app

Device Information

  • Android ID: Used solely for multi-device timer synchronization to prevent duplicate updates. Not shared with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To track and display patching progress
  • To sync data across family devices via Google Firebase (optional)
  • To provide rewards, achievements, and motivational features
  • To enable family sharing functionality
  • To send local notifications (reminders and timer alerts)
  • To respond to feedback submitted through the app

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Android DataStore. If you enable Cloud Sync, data is also stored securely in Google Firebase Cloud Firestore, keyed to your unique profile code. All data transmitted to Firebase is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

5. Third-Party Services

PatchPal uses Google Firebase Cloud Firestore for optional cloud synchronization. Firebase data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. PatchPal does not use any third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Data Sharing

Your data is only shared with family members you explicitly invite using a 6-character profile code. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Children's Privacy

PatchPal is designed for children under parental supervision. We comply with COPPA requirements. All data collection is done with parental consent and oversight. We collect only minimal data necessary for app functionality, do not use data for advertising, and do not share children's data with third parties.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: View all data stored in the app at any time
  • Export: Export your data via Settings
  • Delete: Use "Reset Account" in Settings to delete all data
  • Opt-Out: Disable Cloud Sync or uninstall the app at any time
Medical Disclaimer: PatchPal is a tracking tool only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult qualified healthcare providers regarding eye patching treatment.
