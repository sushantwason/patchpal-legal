## 2. Description of Service

PatchPal is a mobile application designed to help families track and manage eye patching time for children with amblyopia (lazy eye) or other eye conditions requiring patching treatment. The App provides:

- Timer functionality for tracking patching sessions
- Progress tracking and statistics
- Mini-games and activities to engage children during patching
- Achievement and reward systems
- Data export capabilities
- Cloud synchronization across devices (optional)
