Use of the App

PatchPal is designed to help track eye patching therapy for amblyopia (lazy eye). The app is intended to:

  • Track patching time and progress
  • Provide motivation and rewards for children
  • Enable family members to coordinate patching schedules

  • Log visual acuity measurements
