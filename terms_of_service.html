Use of the App

PatchPal is designed to help track eye patching therapy for amblyopia (lazy eye). The app is intended to:

  • Track patching time and progress
  • Provide motivation and rewards for children
  • Enable family members to coordinate patching schedules

Profile Codes and Family Sharing

PatchPal uses a profile code (in PATCH-XXXXXX format) to allow multiple family members to access a child's patching data across devices. By sharing your profile code, you grant the recipient full access to view and modify the associated profile, sessions, and encouragement messages. You are responsible for keeping your profile code secure and sharing it only with trusted family members or caretakers. We are not responsible for unauthorised access resulting from a shared or disclosed profile code.

iCloud Sync and Data Storage

If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is stored in Apple's CloudKit service and may be accessed from any device signed in with the same profile code. Data sync relies on an active internet connection and iCloud availability. We are not responsible for data loss resulting from iCloud service interruptions, account changes, or device issues. Session history older than one year may be automatically removed to manage storage.
